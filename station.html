AMBUR RAILWAY STATION

  • The railway station is an area where the trains stop to leave and board passengers and goods.
  • One railway station can be found out in every city and village.
  • A railway station basically constitutes of platforms, ticket counter, waiting room, and railway building
  • Railway stations are called junctions when routes to several destinations diverge from that station.
  • Railway station is full of passengers, workers, hawkers, and shopkeepers.
